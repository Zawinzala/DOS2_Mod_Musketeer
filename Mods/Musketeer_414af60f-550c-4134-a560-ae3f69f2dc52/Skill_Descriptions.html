• Musketeer Rifle Passive

    Ammunition

    Rifle-Attacks consume Ammunition, requiring you to reload when empty, which mutes, disarmes and slows you by 75% until your next turn. Basic attacks consume 1 Ammunition, skills have individual costs which are noted in their description.

    The current and maximum amount of Ammunition can be checked in your characters inventory. (Item charges)

    Mark interaction

    Rifle based skills can have special interactions with Marked characters, these interactions are also detailed on the individual skills description.

  • Steady Shot

    Fire off a shot with exceptional aim, exceeding normal range and dealing [1] damage

    Consumes 1 Ammunition

    Deals 25% bonus damage against Marked targets

  • Final Act

    Fire off your last remaining round, dealing [1]

    Only useable when you have exactly 1 Ammunition left. Consumes 1 Ammunition

    Deals 60% bonus damage against Marked targets

    If this skill kills a target, set Hasted for 1 turn and recover 1 AP. Killing a Marked target replaces Hasted with Euphoria

    Euphoria increases movement speed by 3m per AP, grants 2 AP recovery and 1 AP per turn. Additionally grants 25% Critical Chance and heals for 20% of your maximum vitality.

  • Reload

    Fully restore your Ammunition next turn. Set Muted, Disarmed and Slowed by 75% until your next turn.

    Fully restores Ammunition at the beginning of your next turn

  • Rapid Fire

    Fire off 3 shots in rapid succession, quickly rechambering 1 round inbetween. Each shot deals [1] damage.

    Each shot heals for 10% of your maximum vitality upon hitting a marked target.

    Consumes 2 Ammunition

  • Buckshot

    Fire off a high caliber explosive round, dealing [1] damage and causing your target to get knocked back.
    Damages all enemies behind the target for [2] damage and creates a fire surface.

    Knocks down marked characters for 1 turn

    Consumes 2 Ammunition

  • Death Sentence

    Mark a target, lowering it's resistances and reducing it's dodge chance.

    Marks target character

  • Covering Fire

    Fire off all your current Ammunition in a area around your target, each dealing [1] damage.
    The scatter radius depends on your distance to the target. The greater the distance, the bigger the scatter radius.

    Consumes all remaining Ammunition

  • Rend the Marked

    Fire off a splitting shot, attacking up to 3 Marked Characters around you, Dealing [1].

    Consumes 1 Ammunition

    Only targets Marked targets

    Killing a target with this skill grants 1 Rend Stack for 2 turns. There is no stack limit.

    Each Rend Stack increases your Finesse, Wits and Initiative by 2 points and boosts your Damage by 7%.

  • Blazing Flare

    Fire a Flare into the air, which explodes at peak altitude and applies Marked to all enemies in it's area. Reveals invisible enemies.

    Consumes 1 Ammunition

    Marks all enemies in it's area

  • Tracking Shot

    Fire a tracking round on a target, dealing [1] damage and Marking it.

    Consumes 1 Ammunition

    Marks target character

  • Throw Trip Wire Flare

    Throw a Trip Wire Flare trap at target location. Trap takes a turn to activate. When active, trap will explode when a character approaches it and Mark it.

    Marks affected character

  • Seeker Strikedown

    Fire off 7 deadly seeker-missiles on every single Marked enemy character around you, each dealing 60% damage in a small area around it's impact location.

    Missiles are only cast on marked targets

  • Steadfast

    Increases the range of a targeted character's ranged attacks and skills by 2m. Also increases accuracy by 10% and Constitution by 5.

    Decreases movement speed by 75% while active.

  • Reload - Incendiary

    Reload your rifle with incendiary Ammunition, causing your basic attacks and rifle based skills to deal bonus fire damage.
    Killing a enemy with this type of Ammunition will cause the target to errupt in a fiery explosion, damaging all enemies around it and igniting them.

  • Reload - Shock

    Reload your rifle with explosive tip Ammunition, causing your basic attacks to inflict bleeding.
    Rifle based skills will cause a small explosion on impact.

  • Reload - Freezing

    Reload your rifle with freezing Ammunition, causing your basic attacks and rifle based skills to deal bonus water damage.
    Rifle based skills will cause a ice surface behind the impact and have a chance to freeze enemies.

  • Reload - Piercing

    Reload your rifle with experimental Doom Ammunition, causing your basic attacks to deal greatly increased physical damage.
    Rifle based skills will cause big explosions on impact.

  • Talent - Executioner

    Gain 2 AP after dealing a killing blow.

    Additionally restores 1 Ammunition when a Rifle is equipped.
